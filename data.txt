### **Relationships**
1. **`TWNDEBT` ↔ `TWNDEBT_RESP_PRSN`**
   - Debt Year (`DEBT_YR`) and Sequence Number (`DEBT_SQ_NMB`) link debts to responsible persons.

2. **`TWNRESP_PRSN_DBTR` ↔ `TWNDEBT_RESP_PRSN`**
   - `UPI` connects responsible persons to their unique identifiers.

3. **`TWNDEBT_RESP_PRSN` ↔ `TWNDEBT_MATCH_REQ`**
   - Debt Year, Sequence Number, and `UPI` link debt responsibilities to match requests.

4. **`TWNDRT_PYMT` ↔ `TWNRPM_AUDIT_TRAIL`**
   - Receipt Adjustment Number (`RCPT_ADJMT_NMB`) links payments to their audit trail.

5. **`TWNRPM_AUDIT_TRAIL` ↔ `TWNDEBT_TOP_COLL`**
   - Timestamp (`RPM_ADT_TRL_TS`) connects audit trails to top-level collections.

6. **`TWNDBTR_DTLS` ↔ `TWNRESP_PRSN_DBTR`**
   - `UPI` ties debtor details to the responsible person.
