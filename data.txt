To create a **Daily Progress Tracker** that shows the full picture of each **Part Detail's** progress from **Start Date** to **End Date**, follow these steps:

---

### **1. Create a New Sheet: "Daily Progress Tracker"**
**Columns to Add:**
1. **Date** (Auto-filled for every day from Start to End Date)
2. **Part Details** (From "Reports")
3. **Owner** (From "Reports")
4. **Start Date** (From "Reports")
5. **End Date** (From "Reports")
6. **% Complete (Yesterday)** (Previous day's completion)
7. **% Complete (Today)** (From "Reports")
8. **Progress Made** (Difference between Today and Yesterday)

---

---

### **2. Automatically Generate Daily Dates for Each Part Detail**
1. Go to **Column A (Date)** in the "Daily Progress Tracker" sheet.

2. **Formula to Generate Dates:**
   ```excel
   =SEQUENCE(VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 2, FALSE) - VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 1, FALSE)+1, , VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 1, FALSE))
   ```

**Explanation:**
- **Start Date:** `VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 1, FALSE)` (Column R)
- **End Date:** `VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 2, FALSE)` (Column S)
- `SEQUENCE(...)`: Automatically generates all dates between Start and End Date.

---

---

### **3. Extract % Complete Data from "Reports"**
#### **% Complete (Today):**
```excel
=IF(AND(A2 >= VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 1, FALSE), 
        A2 <= VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$R:$S, 2, FALSE)),
    VLOOKUP([@[Part Details]], Reports!$E:$L, 7, FALSE), 
    "")
```

**Explanation:**
- **Condition:** Checks if todayâ€™s date (**A2**) is between **Start Date** and **End Date** from "Reports".
- **Result:** If within range, fetch **% Complete** from column **L** of "Reports"; otherwise, leave blank.

---

---

### **4. Calculate Previous Day % Complete**
#### **% Complete (Yesterday):**
```excel
=IFERROR(INDEX(F$2:F2, MATCH([@[Part Details]] & A2-1, C$2:C2 & A$2:A2, 0)), 0)
```

**Explanation:**
- **`MATCH()`**: Finds the corresponding **Part Details** and **Previous Date**.
- **`INDEX()`**: Returns the **% Complete (Today)** from the matching row.
- **`IFERROR(..., 0)`**: If thereâ€™s no previous day entry, returns `0%`.

---

---

### **5. Calculate Progress Made**
#### **Progress Made Formula:**
```excel
=[@[% Complete (Today)]] - [@[% Complete (Yesterday)]]
```

**Explanation:**
- **Today - Yesterday:** Calculates how much progress was made on the current date.

---

---

### **6. Create a Summary View for Leads**

   - **Rows:** `Part Details`
   - **Columns:** `Date`
   - **Values:**
     - **Sum of Progress Made** (Daily Progress)
     - **Max of % Complete (Today)** (Latest completion percentage)
